Our Products

We have long experience in our field, so we can provide you with excellent services.

Outdoor Signage

We manufacture road signs of all kinds, Unipole, Mega Sign, and advertising screens on top of buildings, starting with the design, then manufacturing, installation, making concrete foundations, installing in the building, making internal or external lighting, and installing the advertising message, whether it is a banner or flex faith, and all of that while maintaining the best vision for the advertising site. Calculate weather factors and wind pressure.

pylon

We manufacture activity panels in various forms according to the designs provided, then implement and adhere to all standards and technical specifications necessary to produce a high-quality product..

Cladding

We make special cladding and chassis for car fuel stations and commercial and government storefronts using the highest local and imported materials according to the design and customer requirements..

Lightbox Acrylic and Vinyle

We manufacture and install information signs and illuminated lightboxes, whether the advertising message is Flex Faith printed or acrylic with print and cut vinyl, using Easy Open sections for easy maintenance..

Stands

We manufacture various documents according to design and industry principles.

Flags

We manufacture all types of media, whether static or mobile, and print the necessary materials on high-quality materials according to the required design..

Stainless

We manufacture logos and letters in various shapes from stainless steel, sheet metal, acrylic, and 3D models made of fiberglass. .

Steel Structure and Gates

- We supply and install metal structures according to the engineering drawing (car supply station canopies - truss - advertising unipole - iron fences - gates)
- Making the necessary concrete foundations, and if the installation is on top of buildings and structures, the installation is done by placing the foundations using epoxy.
- Painting using epoxy paints or anti-rust primers
- Plastering using corrugated sheet, galvanized or colored sheet, and sandwich
panels for roof and insulation works, and making the necessary slopes for the water drainage process, while adhering to all standards, conditions, and safety to produce a high-quality product in the shortest possible time.
